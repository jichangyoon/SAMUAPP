How It Works

Three stakeholders. One flywheel.

CREATOR
Upload your meme
Submit original meme to the community contest. Community votes with tokens to pick the best.
Earns: 45% of goods revenue — proportional to votes received
VOTER
Stake tokens to vote
Use community SPL tokens to vote for the best memes. Shape what becomes the official IP.
Earns: 40% of goods revenue — proportional to tokens staked
TEAM
Run the protocol
Winning meme is automatically archived on-chain, printed via Printful, and listed as goods.
Earns: 15% — sustains the protocol infrastructure
When a customer buys the goods → Anchor escrow releases → SOL hits all three wallets simultaneously
